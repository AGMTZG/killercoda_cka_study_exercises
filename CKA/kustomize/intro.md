### Advanced Kustomize Deployment

In this exercise, you will use Kustomize to manage Kubernetes resources across multiple environments.  
You will configure development and production environments with overlays, patches, ConfigMaps, Secrets, environment variables, and resource settings.  

By the end, you will know how to structure a flexible multi-environment Kustomize deployment that adapts to different requirements without duplicating manifests.
